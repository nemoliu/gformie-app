Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Introduction

FormCraft ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use FormCraft for Google Forms.

Information We Collect

Google Account Data: When you sign in with Google, we access your name, email address, and profile photo solely to display within the app. We also request access to Google Forms, Google Forms Responses (read-only), and Google Drive (file-level) to provide the app's core functionality.

Form Data: All form creation, editing, and response data is transmitted directly between your device and Google's servers via the Google Forms API. We do not store, copy, or process your form data on any server we operate.

Subscription Data: In-app purchases and subscription status are managed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not collect or store payment information.

Device Data: We do not collect device identifiers, IP addresses, or analytics data. The app uses Apple's os_log for local diagnostics only, which never leaves your device.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you with Google services and display your profile
  • To create, edit, and manage Google Forms on your behalf
  • To fetch and display form responses from Google's servers
  • To send local push notifications when new responses are detected

Data Storage

FormCraft does not operate backend servers. All data flows directly between your device and Google's APIs. Authentication tokens are stored securely in the iOS Keychain. Cached preferences (such as notification settings) are stored locally on your device using UserDefaults.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In & Google APIs: Subject to Google's Privacy Policy
  • Apple StoreKit: Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties. Your data is only transmitted to Google's servers as required to provide the app's functionality.

Data Retention

We do not retain any user data on our servers. When you sign out of the app, your local cached data is cleared. Your form data remains on Google's servers per Google's data retention policies.

Children's Privacy

FormCraft is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

You can revoke FormCraft's access to your Google account at any time by visiting Google Account Permissions. You can delete the app and all local data at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at formcraft.app@gmail.com.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign in?

FormCraft uses Google Sign-In. Tap "Sign in with Google" and authorize access to your Google Forms, Responses, and Drive. This allows FormCraft to create and manage forms on your behalf.

Is FormCraft an official Google app?

No. FormCraft is an independent third-party app that uses the official Google Forms API to help you manage your forms on iOS. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by Google.

What can I do for free?

Free users can create unlimited forms, use 18+ templates, preview and publish forms, view response summaries (up to 50 responses), import from files/camera/clipboard, and receive push notifications.

What does Pro include?

Pro unlocks QR code sharing, CSV export, full response analytics (by question and individual), custom templates, custom themes, Google Sheets linking, print forms, kiosk mode, and watermark removal.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings tab > tap "Restore Purchases". If you subscribed with a different Apple ID, make sure you're signed in with the correct account.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the iOS Settings app > tap your name > Subscriptions > FormCraft Pro > Cancel Subscription. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.

Why can't I see all my responses?

Free users can view summaries based on the first 50 responses. Upgrade to Pro to see all responses and access the "By Question" and "Individual" views.

How does the camera import work?

FormCraft uses Apple's Vision framework to scan paper documents, extract text via OCR, and automatically detect questions. It supports multiple pages and recognizes various question formats.

Is my data secure?

Yes. FormCraft does not operate any backend servers. All data flows directly between your device and Google's servers. Authentication tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain. See our Privacy Policy for details.
